Internship Opportunities at Cognifyz Technologies

Launch your career with a practical, mentorship-driven internship where you'll build production-quality web experiences alongside experienced engineers.

Program Overview

The Cognifyz Web Developer Internship is a concentrated, hands-on program focused on delivering real product experience. Interns work on cross-functional teams, contribute to the codebase, and participate in the full software lifecycle — from design and implementation to testing and deployment.

Program Duration

This is a 4-week, remote-first internship program. Exceptional performers may be extended offers for full-time roles.

Why Join Cognifyz?

Practical Product Experience

Work on features that ship to real users. You’ll learn code review best practices, collaborate with designers and product managers, and gain experience with deployment workflows.

Mentorship & Feedback

Each intern is paired with a senior engineer for regular 1:1 mentorship, technical feedback, and career guidance designed to accelerate your growth.

Desired Skills & Qualifications

  • Pursuing or recently completed a degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field.
  • Solid understanding of HTML5, CSS3, and modern JavaScript (ES6+).
  • Familiarity with a modern front-end framework such as React, Vue, or Svelte.
  • Experience or familiarity with back-end technologies such as Node.js/Express or Python (Django/Flask) is a plus.
  • Practical experience using Git and collaborative development workflows.
  • Excellent communication skills and a strong desire to learn.
